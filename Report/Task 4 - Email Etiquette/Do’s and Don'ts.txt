Do’s:
Do maintain professionalism: Keep your posts respectful and avoid controversial or offensive content.
Do use privacy settings: Set appropriate privacy settings for your accounts to control who sees your posts.
Do engage positively: Share helpful, constructive, and encouraging content.
Do proofread: Check your posts for spelling and grammar errors to maintain professionalism.
Do respect others' privacy: Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information about others without permission.


Don’ts:
Don’t post inappropriate content: Avoid sharing offensive or controversial material.
Don’t overshare personal information: Be mindful of the amount of personal information you share online.
Don’t engage in online arguments: Avoid getting involved in heated debates or arguments on social media.
Don’t use social media during important tasks: Stay focused during academic work or while meeting with others.
Don’t share content without verification: Avoid spreading unverified or fake news.
