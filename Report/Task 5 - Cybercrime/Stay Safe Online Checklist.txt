"Stay Safe Online" Checklist:
Use Strong Passwords: Always use complex passwords with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Use 2FA on platforms that offer it for an extra layer of security.
Be Cautious of Suspicious Emails: Do not click on links or open attachments from unknown or suspicious sources.
Verify Online Transactions: Ensure websites are secure (look for "https" in the URL) before entering payment details.
Keep Your Software Updated: Regularly update your operating system and apps to avoid vulnerabilities.
Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Financial Transactions: Never make online payments or access sensitive information over unsecured public Wi-Fi networks.
Beware of Phishing Scams: If you receive unsolicited emails asking for sensitive information, always verify the authenticity before responding.
Report Suspicious Activities: Report any suspicious cyber activity or fraud to the authorities, such as the National Cyber Crime Portal.
