# Digital-Literacy
## VITyarthi Project
### Task 1 - Presentation
For Task 1, I created a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic using Canva. The infographic covers three key topics: What Digital Literacy is, Useful Digital Tools for Students, and Safe Internet Practices. I included visually engaging elements, such as icons and illustrations, to make the content more accessible and fun.
The most challenging part of the design was selecting the right balance between text and visuals to ensure the message was clear without overcrowding the space. However, I enjoyed the process of finding suitable images to represent the concepts effectively.
This task helped me learn how to present important information in an engaging way while maintaining clarity and simplicity.

### Task 5 - Cybercrime
For Task 5, I researched Phishing, a type of cybercrime in which attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information. Phishing typically occurs through fraudulent emails that deceive individuals into clicking malicious links. The consequences of this crime can include unauthorized access to accounts, identity theft, and financial losses.
I also created a Stay Safe Online checklist with 8 actionable tips to help students avoid online threats, including using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and being cautious of suspicious emails. I included specific advice for financial/UPI safety, such as verifying transactions before entering payment details and avoiding public Wi-Fi when making financial transactions.
This task has raised my awareness about the risks of cybercrime and the importance of practicing good online security habits.
